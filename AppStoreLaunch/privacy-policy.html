Sacred Pathway Driver Hub — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Sacred Pathway ("we," "our," "us") operates the Sacred Pathway Driver Hub mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and the company information you provide (company name, MC number, DOT number, phone number).

Operational data you create. Loads, expenses, driver records, settlements, paystubs, and uploaded documents (rate confirmations, BOLs, receipts, invoices) that you enter or import into the App.

Document images. Document images may be securely transmitted to a third-party AI service (OpenAI) solely for the purpose of extracting structured text data. No data is used for training, and processing is limited to this function only. We do not sell, share, or store scanned documents beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

Device information. Standard diagnostic data (device model, iOS version, crash logs) provided by Apple to help us improve the App.

What we do NOT collect. We do not collect location data, contacts, advertising identifiers, biometric data, or browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the App and deliver core features (paystub generation, expense tracking, document storage).
  • To authenticate you and protect your account.
  • To extract text from documents you scan or upload, using OpenAI's vision-capable models via our secure backend.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve App reliability.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Account and operational data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database) in encrypted form. Documents you upload are stored in Supabase Storage. Locally-created data (drafts, custom fee settings, branding preferences) is stored on your device only.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers that make the App work:

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure.
  • OpenAI — AI text extraction from scanned documents.
  • Apple — App distribution, in-app purchases, and crash diagnostics.

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can edit or delete your company information, loads, expenses, drivers, and documents at any time within the App. To delete your entire account and all associated data, email us at the address below and we will process the deletion within 30 days.

If you are a California resident (CCPA) or in the EU/UK (GDPR), you have additional rights including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and the right to data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for commercial trucking professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and authentication tokens with short lifetimes. No system is perfectly secure; please use a strong, unique password.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we materially change this policy, we will notify you in the App and update the effective date above.
