# How to Host Your Privacy Policy & Terms (FREE, 5 minutes)

Apple requires a public URL for your privacy policy. Here's the fastest free way using GitHub Pages.

## Step 1 — Create a free GitHub account
1. Go to https://github.com/signup
2. Sign up with your email (use demarquishinton@gmail.com).
3. Pick a username (suggested: `sacredpathway`).

## Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** in the top-right → **New repository**.
2. Repository name: `sacred-pathway-legal`
3. Set it to **Public**.
4. Check **"Add a README file"**.
5. Click **Create repository**.

## Step 3 — Upload the legal pages
1. On the repo page, click **Add file** → **Upload files**.
2. Drag in both files from this folder:
   - `privacy-policy.html`
   - `terms-of-service.html`
3. Scroll down → click **Commit changes**.

## Step 4 — Turn on GitHub Pages
1. Click the **Settings** tab (top of repo).
2. In the left sidebar click **Pages**.
3. Under "Build and deployment" → Source: **Deploy from a branch**.
4. Branch: **main** / folder: **/ (root)** → click **Save**.
5. Wait 1–2 minutes. The page will refresh and show:

   `Your site is live at https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/sacred-pathway-legal/`

## Step 5 — Your URLs are now:
- **Privacy Policy:** `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/sacred-pathway-legal/privacy-policy.html`
- **Terms of Service:** `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/sacred-pathway-legal/terms-of-service.html`

Replace `YOUR-USERNAME` with whatever username you picked. Paste the privacy URL into App Store Connect when it asks for one.
