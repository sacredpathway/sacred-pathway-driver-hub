## Description (4,000 characters max)

Sacred Pathway Driver Hub turns your phone into the back office of your trucking business.

Built for owner-operators, small fleets, and dispatchers who are tired of paper shuffles and spreadsheet headaches. Enter a load in under a minute — broker, route, rate, and fuel surcharge — and the math is done for you. Profit per load, rate per mile, and driver pay are calculated as you type.

HERE'S HOW IT WORKS

📝 Log it — Quick-entry form with auto-calculated revenue, rate per mile, and estimated profit.

📊 Track it — Revenue vs. expenses update in real time. Know your profit per load before you finalize anything.

💸 Pay it — Driver settlements calculate automatically. Factoring fees, authority fees, dispatcher cut, maintenance reserve — all configurable to your exact setup.

📤 Send it — Export a branded paystub PDF. Email it, AirDrop it, save it to Files. Done.

✨ Coming soon — Auto-scan that fills the form for you from a photo of the rate con.

FEATURES
✅ Fast manual load entry — minimal typing, smart defaults
✅ Edit and delete past loads with one swipe
✅ Real-time profit tracking per load (rate per mile, net profit)
✅ Automatic driver settlement calculations
✅ Configurable fees: driver pay %, dispatcher cut, factoring, authority, maintenance reserve
✅ Toggle any fee between dollar amount and percentage
✅ Add custom fees and rename anything
✅ Branded paystub PDFs with your logo and company info
✅ Manual paystub creator for offline jobs
✅ Save unfinished payrolls as drafts
✅ Loads, expenses, and drivers organized by date and broker
✅ Weekly totals and average rate-per-mile summaries

WHO IT'S FOR
• Owner-operators running 1–5 trucks
• Small carriers managing a handful of drivers
• Dispatchers handling settlement paperwork
• Anyone who's tired of QuickBooks for trucking

## Review Notes (for the App Store reviewer — internal, not public)

Sacred Pathway is a business productivity app for commercial truckers. Test account credentials are provided in the Demo Account fields of App Store Connect.

To test the core flow:
1. Sign in with the test account
2. Tap "Add a Load" — fill in broker, route, and rate. Watch the revenue, rate per mile, and profit calculate automatically.
3. Tap More → Operations → Create Paystub Manually to see the manual paystub flow.
4. Settings → Fee Percentages to see configurable driver settlement fees.
5. Settings → Branding to see logo customization for exported paystubs.

This version uses manual entry only — no on-device or server-side AI processing, no OCR, no third-party scanning services. Documents/receipts are stored in the user's Supabase account. Auto-scan is a planned future feature and will be disclosed separately if and when it ships.

No social features, no UGC visible to other users, no sensitive content.
