## Description (4,000 characters max)

Sacred Pathway Driver Hub turns your phone into the back office of your trucking business.

Built for owner-operators, small fleets, and dispatchers who are tired of paper shuffles and spreadsheet headaches. Snap or upload any rate confirmation, BOL, fuel receipt, lumper fee, toll, or repair bill — AI reads it instantly, organizes it, and tracks your true profit per load.

HERE'S HOW IT WORKS

📸 Scan it — Point your camera at any rate con, fuel receipt, lumper fee, toll, or repair bill. AI extracts everything instantly.

📁 Upload it — Got a PDF emailed from your broker? A rate con saved in your Files? Import it directly — from your photo library, Files app, email, or anywhere on your phone. AI reads it the same way.

📊 Track it — Revenue vs. expenses update in real time. Know your profit per load before you finalize anything.

💸 Pay it — Driver settlements calculate automatically. Factoring fees, authority fees, dispatcher cut, maintenance reserve — all configurable to your exact setup.

📤 Send it — Export a branded paystub PDF. Email it, AirDrop it, save it to Files. Done.

FEATURES
✅ AI-powered scanning of rate cons, BOLs, receipts, and invoices
✅ Upload documents from Files, Photos, or email
✅ PDF & image import — rate cons, BOLs, receipts, invoices
✅ Real-time profit tracking per load
✅ Automatic driver settlement calculations
✅ Configurable fees: driver pay %, dispatcher cut, factoring, authority, maintenance reserve
✅ Toggle any fee between dollar amount and percentage
✅ Add custom fees and rename anything
✅ Branded paystub PDFs with your logo and company info
✅ Manual paystub creator for offline jobs
✅ Save unfinished payrolls as drafts
✅ Searchable document vault by load, broker, or date
✅ Smart Insights — AI summaries and rate intelligence

## Review Notes (for the App Store reviewer — internal, not public)

Sacred Pathway is a business productivity app for commercial truckers. Test account credentials are provided in the Demo Account fields of App Store Connect.

To test the core flow:
1. Sign in with the test account
2. Tap "Scan" — you can use any image from your photo library to test extraction
3. Tap More → Operations → Create Paystub Manually to see the manual flow
4. Settings → Fee Percentages to see configurable fees
5. Settings → Branding to see logo customization

The app uses OpenAI's vision-capable models (via the OpenAI API, called from a secure Supabase Edge Function on our backend — never from the device) for document text extraction. No user data is used for training. This is disclosed in the privacy policy.

No social features, no UGC visible to other users, no sensitive content.
