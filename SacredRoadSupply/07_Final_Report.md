## C. Revenue Projections

Assumptions: $300–$500/mo ad spend, $45 AOV (free-ship-over-$75 lifts AOV from $35 baseline), 22% net margin after COGS + ads + Shopify fees.

| Scenario | Month 1 Rev | Month 1 Profit | Month 3 Rev | Month 3 Profit | Month 6 Rev | Month 6 Profit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Low** | $80–$150 | -$200 to -$350 (still in red — normal) | $200–$400 | -$50 to $0 | $400–$700 | $40–$100 |
| **Medium** | $200–$350 | -$100 to -$200 | $600–$1,100 | $100–$200 | $1,500–$3,000 | $300–$600 |
| **High** | $500–$800 | $30–$130 | $1,500–$2,800 | $300–$600 | $5,000–$10,000 | $1,000–$2,000 |

**Reality check:**
- Months 1–2: nearly all dropship stores lose money learning ad creative. Plan for that.
- Month 3 is the first realistic break-even checkpoint.
- Niche advantage: trucking has cheap CPMs ($8–$12) and high LTV (returning buyer rate ~22% on accessory stores). Edge here vs. broad DTC.
- Niche risk: ~3.5M US truckers total. You'll exhaust cheap reach by month 6 if you don't broaden to RV / overland / blue-collar adjacent.

**Profit-multiplier paths once stable (~month 4+):**
1. Add Sacred Road Supply branded apparel (Trendsi) — 70–80% margins, identity-driven repeat buyers.
2. Add Driver Hub Pro cross-promo: $5 store credit with annual subscription. Drives both LTVs.
3. Fleet/bulk pricing channel via `fleet@sacredpathway.org` — even one 10-truck fleet order = $1,000+.
4. Subscription box ("Trucker Supply Crate") — $39/mo curated. 2026's hottest commerce model in niche.
