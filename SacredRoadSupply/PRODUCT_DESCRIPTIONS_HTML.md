## Why These Descriptions Work

- **First paragraph = the benefit, in driver voice.** Not "Elevate your driving experience" — "Stop idling to stay warm."
- **Bullets are specific, not generic.** Real numbers, real fitment, real concerns.
- **Specs are honest.** Materials, dimensions, what's in the box. No exaggeration.
- **"Built For" tag block** mirrors collection-page filtering so SEO crawlers see the cluster.
- **Shipping/Returns block** sets expectations and reduces support tickets.
- **FAQ schema** — Shopify can extract the Q&A pairs for structured-data rich snippets, boosting Google SERP appearance.

All 20 descriptions are token-discipline-compliant (no `srsGoldHighlight` references — they're text-only).
