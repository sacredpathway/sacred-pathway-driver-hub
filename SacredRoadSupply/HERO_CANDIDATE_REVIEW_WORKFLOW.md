## Step 2 — The brief (what to look for)

A candidate photo is a strong yes if it has:

✅ **Composition:**
- Truck cab interior OR highway dawn shot
- Visible road or dashboard providing depth
- Left third of frame relatively clean (room for headline overlay)

✅ **Mood:**
- Quiet, early-morning or sunset light
- Golden warm highlights + cool blue shadows
- Feels real, not staged

✅ **Technical:**
- Sharp focus
- Available at high resolution (≥ 3000 px wide on Unsplash)
- No people in the foreground (face-focused photos pull attention away from gear)
- No visible carrier branding/logos on the truck (legal risk)

❌ **Auto-reject if it has:**
- People in suits or "happy truck driver" stock poses
- Bright midday lighting
- Recognizable carrier livery (Schneider, Werner, etc.)
- Heavy lens flare obscuring detail
- Cars (we sell to truck drivers, not motorists)

## Step 4 — I'll review and pick (my work, ~20 min)

For each candidate I'll evaluate against the brief above:
- Composition fit (will the headline + CTAs sit comfortably on the left third?)
- Mood fit (does it match Sacred Road Supply's working-driver voice?)
- Crop tolerance (can it crop to both 2880×1200 desktop AND 1080×1350 mobile without losing the subject?)
- Brand integrity (does the gold/green palette grade onto this image naturally?)

I'll return a ranked recommendation:

```
PICK: Candidate #2
WHY: [3-sentence rationale]
DESKTOP CROP COORDS: x=420, y=100, w=4200, h=1750  (then resize to 2880×1200)
MOBILE CROP COORDS: x=1800, y=300, w=1620, h=2025  (then resize to 1080×1350)
COLOR GRADE SETTINGS: highlights +10 warm, shadows -8 cool, saturation -5, contrast +8

BACKUP: Candidate #4 (if #2's license is restricted)

REJECT REASONS:
- #1: subject is centered — no room for headline overlay
- #3: cars visible — wrong audience signal
- #5: midday lighting — wrong mood
```
