## 2. Best Supplier Recommendation for Sacred Road Supply

### Primary: **Zendrop — Standard Pro at $49/mo** (or Usage-Based starting at $29/mo)

**Why:**
- Largest US-warehoused private-label catalog of trucker-relevant hard goods (LED lights, phone mounts, blankets, charging gear, organizers)
- 2–5 day US shipping matches the brand promise on the homepage
- Branded invoices unlock at Pro — supports "Sacred Road Supply" customer experience from day 1
- Automated fulfillment (no manual order pasting)

**Cost optimization:** Start on **Usage-Based at $29/mo** while linking 1–20 products. Auto-bumps to $49 once you scale to 21+ linked SKUs — same as Pro flat rate. No reason to pay $49/mo before you have inventory live.

### Backup: **CJDropshipping — Free**
- Use for SKUs Zendrop doesn't have (specialty trucker gear, custom-printed accessories)
- Pay-per-order — zero fixed cost
- Manually filter "US Warehouse" on every sourcing request

### Defer to Month 2: **Trendsi (Free)** for branded apparel (caps, tees, hoodies)
- Fashion-only catalog, US-warehoused, 2–7 day shipping
- Adds 60–80% margin SKUs once the brand has identity

### Do NOT use as primary:
- **DSers** — too much China shipping for a brand built on fast delivery
- **Spocket** — high monthly cost + smaller trucker-niche catalog for the price
- **AutoDS** — overpriced relative to what you need at this stage (no real free tier)

### A. Shopify Basic Plan

**Cost:** $39/mo monthly billing OR $29/mo on annual (25% savings)
**Transaction fees with Shopify Payments:** 2.9% + $0.30 per online sale
**Third-party gateway surcharge:** +2% on top of gateway fees
**Source:** [Shopify pricing](https://www.shopify.com/pricing)

**Setup steps:**
1. Go to `shopify.com/free-trial`
2. Email: `demarquishinton@gmail.com`
3. Store name: `Sacred Road Supply`
4. After 3-day trial, select **Basic — Annual ($29/mo, $348/yr)**
5. Skip Shopify-recommended apps during onboarding (we'll install manually)

### B. Shopify Tax

**Pricing (2026):** Free until your store hits the sales threshold (stores created after May 13, 2026 have a lifetime threshold, not annual). After threshold, 0.35% per order, capped at $0.99/order, max $5,000/yr per region.
**Source:** [Shopify Tax pricing](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/taxes/shopify-tax/pricing)

**Setup steps:**
1. Shopify admin → Settings → Taxes and duties → United States
2. Click **Set up Shopify Tax** (default service)
3. Add business address: Greensboro, AL 36744
4. Shopify auto-detects state nexus and calculates rooftop tax at checkout
5. Optional: Enable "Collect tax in additional states" if you anticipate nexus elsewhere (revisit at $100k/yr revenue)

### C. Shopify Payments

**Setup steps (needs Demarquis to log in + provide):**
1. Shopify admin → Settings → Payments → Activate Shopify Payments
2. Business info:
   - Business type: **LLC**

## 4. DNS Setup — `shop.sacredpathway.org` from Squarespace

**Verified against:** [Shopify subdomain connect docs](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/domains/add-a-domain/connecting-domains/connect-subdomain), [Squarespace-to-Shopify guide](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/domains/add-a-domain/connecting-domains/connecting-to-squarespace)

### Step 1 — In Squarespace

1. Log into Squarespace → **Home** → **Settings** → **Domains**
2. Click `sacredpathway.org` → **DNS Settings**
3. Scroll to **Custom Records** → click **Add Record**
4. Add this ONE record (do **not** modify root A records, www, or anything else):

| Host | Type | Data |
|---|---|---|
| `shop` | `CNAME` | `shops.myshopify.com` |

5. Save

### Step 2 — In Shopify

1. Shopify admin → **Settings** → **Domains**
2. Click **Connect existing domain**
3. Type: `shop.sacredpathway.org`
4. Click **Next**
5. Shopify auto-verifies the CNAME (usually 5 min – 1 hr, occasionally up to 48 hr per Shopify docs)
6. If Shopify requests additional verification, it will display a TXT record to add back in Squarespace — follow that prompt if shown
7. After verification, Shopify auto-provisions **Let's Encrypt SSL** (5–60 min)

### Step 3 — Primary Domain Choice

- Leave `sacredroadsupply.myshopify.com` as **primary** during build (so admin works during DNS prop)
- **Once verified and SSL live, switch primary to `shop.sacredpathway.org`**
- Shopify auto-301's the `.myshopify.com` URL to the primary

### Step 4 — Verify

1. Open private/incognito browser
2. Visit `https://shop.sacredpathway.org`
3. Expect: store loads, green padlock, no certificate warning
4. If errors, wait 60 min and retry. If still failing after 24 hr, re-check the CNAME in Squarespace.

**Important:** This setup leaves `sacredpathway.org` and `www.sacredpathway.org` (your existing Squarespace freight carrier site) completely untouched.
