# Sacred Road Supply — First 30 Days Social Media Calendar

**Platforms:** Instagram + Facebook (cross-posted) · TikTok (where Reels work) · X/Twitter (when relevant)
**Cadence:** 5 posts/week, M–F. Weekends rest.
**Goal:** Build identity + drive first-month traffic to shop.sacredpathway.org without burning ad budget.
**Voice rule:** Working-driver, direct, no corporate filler. Never use "synergy," "elevate," "game-changer," or any emoji that isn't a flag/truck/wrench.

## Monthly Engagement Plan (parallel to posts)

| Action | Cadence | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Reply to every comment within 4 hr during business hours | Daily | You |
| Engage with 20-30 driver/trucker accounts (likes + meaningful comments) | Daily | You |
| Save 3-5 saves of driver content into a "UGC pipeline" folder | Daily | You |
| DM 5 trucker creators with reach 5k-50k offering a free product trial | Weekly | You |
| Repost stories from customers who tag the brand | As they happen | You |
| Audit which posts hit, which flopped | End of month | You |

## Performance Benchmarks (industry baseline for a new D2C brand)

| Metric | Week 1 | Week 4 |
|---|---|---|
| Followers gained | 20-50 | 150-300 |
| Avg post reach | 100-300 | 500-1,500 |
| Avg post engagement rate | 2-4% | 3-6% |
| Profile visits → website clicks | 5-15 | 30-80 |
| Direct attributable orders from organic social | 0-2 | 3-10 |

Anything below low end = creative or hashtags need work. Above high end = you've got a hit; double the cadence.

---

## What To Post Next (Month 2 Sketch)

Once you've seen what hits in Month 1, build Month 2 around what worked:

- 2x the format that got the most engagement (Reels are usually it)
- A "behind the scenes" angle (e.g., shooting product, packing orders, family truck stories)
- First customer testimonial repost (assumes Week 4 had reviews come in)
- Black Friday tease if month 2 lands near November

Save Month 1 metrics in a Notion or Google Doc — those numbers compound into Month 3 strategy.
