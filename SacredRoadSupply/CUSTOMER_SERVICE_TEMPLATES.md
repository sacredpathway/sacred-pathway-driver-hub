# Sacred Road Supply — Customer Service Email Templates

**Use:** When first orders arrive, replies have to be fast and on-brand. These templates cover the 6 most common inbound emails. Copy → paste → personalize the brackets → send.

**Sender:** `hello@sacredpathway.org`
**Reply-to:** same (or set up `support@sacredpathway.org` as alias and route to same inbox)
**Tone:** Working-driver voice. Direct. No corporate filler. Brief.

---

## Template 1 — Order Acknowledgment (manual, beyond Shopify's automatic confirmation)

**Use when:** A customer emails with a question right after ordering, or for first-time-buyer personal touch on high-AOV orders ($75+).

**Subject:** `Got your order — thanks from Greensboro`

```

Just wanted to say thanks for the order. Real human here — Demarquis at
Sacred Pathway LLC, the small dry-van carrier in Greensboro, Alabama
that runs this store.

Your gear ships from our U.S. warehouse in 1-2 business days. You'll
get a tracking number by email as soon as it's on the truck.

If you've got any questions, hit reply. I read every email myself.

Drive safe,
Demarquis
Sacred Road Supply
hello@sacredpathway.org
```

**"Does it fit my [Freightliner / Kenworth / Peterbilt / Volvo / Mack]?"**

> pickup, it works there too.

**"Can I get fleet pricing?"**
> Yes. Email fleet@sacredpathway.org with how many drivers and which
> products. We do volume pricing on 10+ orders and dedicated quotes on
> 50+ orders.

**"Is this DOT compliant?"**

> vest, etc.) meet current FMCSA/FMVSS standards. The product page has
> the specific compliance reference.

**"How long does shipping take?"**
> Ships from a U.S. warehouse in 1-2 business days. Most U.S. orders
> arrive in 3-7 business days total. AK/HI takes 5-10 days.

**"What if it breaks in 2 months?"**
> Most products have a 12-month manufacturer warranty. If something
> fails earlier than expected, email me and I'll either send a
> replacement or full refund — depends on what makes you happiest.

## Negative Review / Public Complaint Response Template

**Use when:** A 1-star or 2-star review is posted on the product page, social, or third-party site.

**Approach:** Respond fast. Own it. Offer real action, not corporate copy.

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME or REVIEW_AUTHOR],

This is Demarquis from Sacred Road Supply. I'm sorry — that's not what
we want for any customer.

I'd like to make this right. Can you reply with your order number? I'll
send a replacement out today and refund what you paid. No questions.

If there's something specific we got wrong with the product itself,
tell me — we're picky about what makes the catalog and feedback like
yours helps us cut the wrong gear.

Either way, the next move is mine. I'll handle it.

hello@sacredpathway.org
```

## Inbox Hygiene Rules (for the human reading these)

- **Respond within 24 hours.** Even "got it, looking into this" counts.
- **Personalize the bracket fields** — never paste a template with `[FIRST_NAME]` still visible.
- **Sign with "Demarquis"** — feels like a real person; "Sacred Road Supply Team" feels corporate.
- **Never argue.** If a customer is wrong, find the policy they didn't see and quote it gently. If they're upset, lead with action.
- **Photograph any damage / fault** — useful for warranty claims with suppliers and helpful for future product audits.
- **Log every recurring complaint** — if 3+ customers say the same thing about a product, that's a signal to drop or swap the SKU.
