## §4 — SHIPPING POLICY (paste into Shopify Settings → Policies → Shipping)

### Where We Ship
Sacred Road Supply ships to all 50 U.S. states, including Alaska and Hawaii, plus APO/FPO/DPO military addresses. We do not currently ship internationally.

### Shipping Rates
- **Standard Shipping (Contiguous 48 states):** FREE on orders $75+. $5.95 flat on orders under $75.
- **Alaska, Hawaii, U.S. Territories:** $14.95 flat.
- **APO/FPO/DPO:** $9.95 flat.
- **Expedited (2–3 business day) Shipping:** $14.95 — available at checkout on eligible items.

### Processing Time
Orders placed before 2:00 PM CT Monday–Friday ship the same business day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM CT, on weekends, or on U.S. federal holidays ship the next business day.

### Delivery Time
- **Standard:** 3–7 business days after shipment.
- **Expedited:** 2–3 business days after shipment.
- **Alaska/Hawaii:** 5–10 business days.

### Tracking
You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships. If you haven't received tracking within 2 business days of ordering, email us at hello@sacredpathway.org and we'll chase it down personally.

### Lost, Stolen, or Damaged Shipments
If your package shows delivered but you can't find it, wait 24 hours (carriers occasionally mark early) then contact us. We'll work with the carrier to file a claim. If damaged in transit, send a photo within 48 hours of delivery and we'll replace it free of charge.

### Incorrect Address
We are not responsible for orders shipped to addresses entered incorrectly at checkout. Please double-check your shipping address before placing your order. We're happy to update your address before the order ships — email us immediately.

## §5 — RETURN POLICY (paste into Shopify Settings → Policies → Refund)

### 30-Day Returns
If you're not satisfied with your order, return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the product price.

### Conditions
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resaleable condition.
- Electronics must include all original cables, manuals, and accessories.
- Personal-care items (compression socks, headsets, etc.) cannot be returned once opened, due to hygiene rules — unless defective.
- Final-sale or clearance items marked on the product page cannot be returned.

### How to Start a Return
Email **returns@sacredpathway.org** with your order number and a brief reason. We'll send you a return label within one business day.

### Return Shipping
- **Defective or wrong items:** We pay return shipping.
- **Buyer's remorse or no-longer-needed:** Buyer pays return shipping. (We can deduct the return label cost from the refund if you'd rather not handle it yourself — just ask.)

### Refunds
Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 3–5 business days after we receive the returned item. You'll get an email confirmation when the refund is processed.

### Exchanges
We don't process direct exchanges. Return the original for a refund and place a new order — it's faster.

## §6 — FAQ

**How fast will my order ship?**
Orders placed before 2:00 PM CT on a business day ship the same day. Most U.S. orders arrive in 3–7 business days.

**Where do you ship from?**
Most orders ship from U.S. warehouses (CA, NJ, GA). A small number of specialty items ship from international warehouses — you'll see the estimated delivery date on the product page before you check out.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Not yet. We currently ship to the 50 U.S. states, U.S. territories, and APO/FPO/DPO military addresses only.

**How do I track my order?**
You'll get an email with a tracking number as soon as your order ships. If it's been more than 2 business days, email hello@sacredpathway.org.

**What's your return policy?**
30 days, full refund on unused items in original packaging. See the Return Policy page for details.

**My item arrived damaged. What do I do?**
Email hello@sacredpathway.org with your order number and a photo within 48 hours of delivery. We'll send a replacement free of charge.

**Are these products DOT compliant?**
Items marked DOT-compliant (reflective triangle kits, hi-vis vests, etc.) meet current FMCSA standards. Read the product page carefully — if you're not sure, email us before you order.

**Do you offer fleet or bulk pricing?**
Yes. Email fleet@sacredpathway.org with what you need and how many drivers — we'll come back with a quote within 24 hours.

**How is Sacred Road Supply related to Sacred Pathway LLC?**
Sacred Road Supply is a brand of Sacred Pathway LLC, a family-owned dry-van freight carrier in Alabama (USDOT 4250348 / MC 1647441). The Driver Hub app at app.sacredpathway.org is also ours.

**Can I pay with PayPal?**
Yes. We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Shop Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal at checkout.

**Do you charge sales tax?**
Yes, where required by law. Sales tax is calculated at checkout based on your shipping address.

**Does the 12V heated blanket fit my truck?**
Yes — it plugs into any standard 12V cigarette-lighter outlet (factory in 99% of semi trucks). The cord is 8 feet long so it reaches the sleeper. Not for 24V-only outlets.

**Will the USB-C charger work in my 24V semi?**
Yes — this model is dual-voltage 12V/24V. Most semi trucks run 24V — confirm before installing in 12V-only vehicles. Standard 12V-only chargers will burn out on a 24V system. Ours won't.
