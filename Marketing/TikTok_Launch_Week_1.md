## Rules of the Road

**Post times:** 6–8am ET (drivers checking phones at the truck stop) and 9–11pm ET (end of day). Pick one slot and stick to it.

**Reply to every comment in the first hour.** That's how you train the algorithm.

**Don't link-in-bio to the App Store until it's live.** Use a simple landing page or a "join the waitlist" form until then. Dead links kill trust.

**Watch the 3-second retention rate.** If a video's below 70%, the hook is wrong — rewrite and reshoot.

**Duet and stitch trucking creators with large followings** (@indianmacktrucker, @freddiesaenz, etc.). Easier to hijack an audience than build one cold.
